The Challenge: Real-time Sync

The primary obstacle was achieving determinism at scale. Biometric sensors generate a continuous stream of packetized data that must be ordered and processed in less than 50 milliseconds to maintain real-time feedback loops for medical practitioners.

The legacy system couldn't handle peak loads during multi-site clinical trials, causing critical data loss and delays in patient monitoring.

99.9% Accuracy Req.
150ms+ Legacy Latency

The Solution

A decentralized microservices mesh built for throughput and reliability.

hub

Event-Driven Mesh

Leveraging Apache Kafka as the nervous system, we decoupled data ingestion from processing. This allowed for horizontal scaling of individual validators without affecting system-wide latency.
